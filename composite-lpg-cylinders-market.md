In contrast, composite material tank winding utilizes lightweight, high-strength materials like carbon fiber or fiberglass, offering improved portability and corrosion resistance. Both types aim to enhance the safety and efficiency of LPG storage and transportation, catering to various consumer preferences and industry demands.</p></p>
