<p><p>Piezoelectric motors and actuators harness the piezoelectric effect, enabling materials to generate mechanical movement when subjected to an electric field. The market is categorized into three main types: ceramics, composites, and polymers. Ceramic piezoelectric materials are widely used for their high efficiency and stability. Composites combine different materials to enhance performance and durability. Polymer piezoelectric devices offer flexibility and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for a variety of applications, including medical devices, automotive systems, and robotics.</p></p>
