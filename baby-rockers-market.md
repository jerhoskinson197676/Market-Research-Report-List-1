<p><p>Baby rockers are categorized into two main market types: electric and manual. Manual rockers, on the other hand, rely on physical methods, such as gentle pushes or gravity, for movement. They are often more portable and require no power source, making them suitable for parents seeking simplicity and ease of use. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in infant care.</p></p>
