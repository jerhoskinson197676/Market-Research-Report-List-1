<p><p>Fully automatic thermoforming machines are categorized mainly into vacuum forming, pneumatic thermoforming, and other types. Vacuum forming utilizes suction to shape heated thermoplastic sheets over molds, creating detailed designs. Pneumatic thermoforming, on the other hand, employs compressed air to expand and mold the material, allowing for thicker parts and complex shapes. Other types may include mechanical or hybrid systems, which integrate different methods for enhanced performance and versatility, catering to diverse industrial applications in packaging and manufacturing.</p></p>
