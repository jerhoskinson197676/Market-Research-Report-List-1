<p><p>The piston pump market can be categorized into four main types: lift pumps, which elevate fluids using suction; force pumps, designed to push fluids under pressure; axial piston pumps, where pistons are aligned parallel to the pump shaft, suitable for high-pressure applications; and radial piston pumps, which have pistons arranged radially around the shaft, offering high torque and efficiency. Each type serves distinct industrial applications, influencing demand and development in sectors such as hydraulics, manufacturing, and automotive.</p></p>
