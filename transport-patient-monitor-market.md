<p><p>The transport patient monitor market is categorized into three types based on patient acuity levels. High-acuity transport monitors are designed for critically ill patients, providing comprehensive parameters like ECG, invasive blood pressure, and multi-gas analysis. Mid-acuity monitors cater to patients with moderate health issues, offering essential monitoring features without the complexity of high-acuity systems. Low-acuity monitors are suited for stable patients requiring basic observations like heart rate and oxygen saturation, focusing on simplicity and portability during transport.</p></p>
