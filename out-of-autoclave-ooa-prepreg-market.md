<p><p>The Out-of-Autoclave (OOA) prepreg market primarily features two types: unidirectional and directional. Unidirectional prepregs consist of fibers aligned in a single direction, enhancing tensile strength and stiffness, making them ideal for structural components. On the other hand, directional prepregs have fibers arranged in various orientations, allowing for tailored mechanical properties and improved performance in complex geometries. Both types are essential in aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods industries, enabling efficient manufacturing and lightweight designs.</p></p>
