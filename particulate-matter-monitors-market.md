<p><p>The particulate matter monitors market includes various technologies such as TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitors, which provide continuous mass measurements of particulate matter through the oscillation of a tapered element. Beta attenuation monitors utilize the attenuation of beta radiation to measure particles, offering real-time data. Other types encompass various methods like optical and laser monitors, which detect particulates based on light scattering. Each type serves different applications and specific measurement needs in air quality monitoring.</p></p>
