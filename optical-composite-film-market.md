<p><p>The Optical Composite Film market includes various types such as DOP (Diffuse Optical Film), POP (Prism Optical Film), MOP (Micro Optical Film), POM (Polyoxymethylene Film), and others. DOP enhances light diffusion for uniform brightness, POP uses prism structures to increase brightness and viewing angles, MOP employs microstructures for precise light control, and POM offers durability and thermal stability. These films are used in displays, lighting, and electronics, catering to diverse optical performance and application needs.</p></p>
