<p><p>The Fixed Automatic Number Plate Recognition (ANPR) Systems market comprises several key components. ANPR cameras capture vehicle license plates with high precision in various lighting conditions. ANPR software processes the images, extracting and analyzing plate data for identification and record-keeping. Frame grabbers facilitate the transfer of captured image data to the computer system for processing. Triggers, such as sensors or loops, activate the cameras when a vehicle approaches, ensuring optimal performance and accurate data capture in real-time scenarios.</p></p>
