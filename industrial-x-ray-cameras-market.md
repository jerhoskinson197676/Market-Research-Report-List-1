<p><p>The Industrial X-Ray Cameras market primarily includes two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors. CCD cameras are known for their high-quality imaging and low noise levels, making them suitable for detailed inspections in industrial applications. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer faster readout speeds, lower power consumption, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for real-time imaging. Both types serve critical roles in quality control, safety inspections, and non-destructive testing across various industries.</p></p>
