<p><p>Triggered spark gaps are crucial components used in electrical circuits to manage voltage surges and protect devices. The market for these components is divided into ceramic spark gaps and metal spark gaps. Ceramic spark gaps are known for their high voltage endurance and thermal stability, making them suitable for high-power applications. Metal spark gaps, on the other hand, offer lower breakdown voltages and faster triggering, making them ideal for pulse applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including telecom, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
