<p><ul><li>Drive Hydraulic Wrench</li><li>Hollow Hydraulic Wrench</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque wrench market is primarily divided into two types: drive hydraulic wrenches and hollow hydraulic wrenches. Drive hydraulic wrenches utilize a socket attachment to apply torque, allowing for efficient fastening and loosening of bolts in confined spaces. Hollow hydraulic wrenches, on the other hand, feature a hollow design to accommodate long bolt lengths and enable hydraulic fluid flow through the wrench, making them ideal for applications in construction and heavy machinery where accessibility is a concern.</p></p>
