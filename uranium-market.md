<p><p>The uranium market features various deposit types, each influenced by geological characteristics. Granite-type uranium deposits originate from granite formations and are typically high-grade but less frequent. Volcanic-type deposits arise from volcanic activity, often associated with hydrothermal processes. Sandstone-type deposits are formed in sedimentary basins and are more widespread, allowing for easier extraction. Carbonate-siliceous-pelitic rock type deposits occur in carbonaceous sediments, offering unique mineralization patterns. Each deposit type affects extraction methods, market dynamics, and pricing within the uranium sector.</p></p>
