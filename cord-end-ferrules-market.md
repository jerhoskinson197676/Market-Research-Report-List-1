<p><p>Cord-end ferrules are essential components in electrical connections, primarily used to terminate and protect wire ends. The market is divided into two main types: insulated and non-insulated ferrules. Insulated ferrules are equipped with a protective coating, enhancing safety and preventing short circuits, making them ideal for applications where exposure to conductive materials occurs. Non-insulated ferrules, lacking this coating, offer simpler termination solutions for environments where insulation isn't a concern, often used in industrial settings for better conductivity.</p></p>
