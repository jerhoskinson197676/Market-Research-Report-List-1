<p><p>The Nuclear Magnetic Resonance (NMR) hardware market comprises several key components. Magnets generate a strong magnetic field essential for NMR. Field locks stabilize this field for accuracy. Shim coils fine-tune the field homogeneity, enhancing signal quality. Sample probes hold the materials being analyzed. RF coils transmit and receive radiofrequency signals, crucial for data collection. Gradient coils allow spatial encoding of signals to differentiate between different locations in the sample. Quadrature detectors improve signal detection and analysis through phase detection techniques.</p></p>
