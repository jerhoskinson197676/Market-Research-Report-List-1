<p><p>The polypropylene industrial tapes market is primarily categorized into two types: single-sided tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided tape features an adhesive on one side, making it ideal for securing, insulating, or bundling materials. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive coatings on both sides, allowing for bonding two surfaces together seamlessly. Both types are widely used across various industries, such as packaging, automotive, and construction, owing to their durability, flexibility, and versatility in applications that require strong adhesion.</p></p>
