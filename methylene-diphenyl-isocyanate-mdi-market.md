<p><p>Methylene Diphenyl Isocyanate (MDI) is a key chemical used in the production of polyurethane foams and coatings. The MDI market is primarily categorized into two types: pure MDI and polymeric MDI. Pure MDI is characterized by its high viscosity and is used in rigid foam applications, offering excellent thermal insulation. Polymeric MDI, on the other hand, has a broader molecular weight distribution and is commonly utilized in flexible foams, elastomers, and adhesives, providing enhanced flexibility and durability.</p></p>
