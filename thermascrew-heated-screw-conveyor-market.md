<p><p>The Thermascrew Heated Screw Conveyor market is segmented into two main types: electric heating and media thermal regulation. Electric heating involves direct integration of electric heating elements within the conveyor system, providing precise temperature control and efficient heating for materials. Media thermal regulation, on the other hand, uses a heated medium, like oil or water, to maintain optimal temperatures through the conveyor, ensuring uniform heat transfer to the materials being processed. Both methods enhance process efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
