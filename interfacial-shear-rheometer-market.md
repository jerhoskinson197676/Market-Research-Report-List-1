<p><ul><li>Cone Plate Rheometer</li><li>Concentric Cylinder Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interfacial shear rheometer market includes two main types: cone plate rheometers and concentric cylinder rheometers. Cone plate rheometers utilize a rotating cone atop a flat plate to measure flow behavior at interfaces, making them ideal for studying thin films and emulsions. Concentric cylinder rheometers involve a cylindrical sample placed between two coaxial cylinders, effectively measuring shear stress and viscosity. Both types provide critical insights into material properties and behavior, catering to diverse applications in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
