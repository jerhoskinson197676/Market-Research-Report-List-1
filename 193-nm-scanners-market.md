<p><ul><li>193 nm Dry Scanners</li><li>193 nm Wet Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 193 nm scanners market is divided into two main types: dry scanners and wet scanners. Dry scanners use a photoresist layer that does not require a liquid development process, making them simpler and faster for semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, wet scanners utilize a liquid immersion solution to enhance resolution and image quality, allowing for finer details in chip fabrication. Each type caters to different manufacturing needs and technologies, impacting efficiency, cost, and performance in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
