<p><p>Thermopile detectors are categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types based on their sensitivity and application. Single-channel detectors operate with one sensing element, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for basic temperature measurements. In contrast, multi-channel detectors feature multiple sensing elements, enabling simultaneous temperature readings from different locations. This capability enhances precision and allows for intricate thermal analysis in applications such as industrial monitoring, medical diagnostics, and environmental sensing, catering to diverse market needs across various sectors.</p></p>
