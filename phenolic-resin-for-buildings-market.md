<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin For Buildings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Phenolic Resin</li><li>Thermosetting Phenolic Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phenolic resins for buildings are categorized primarily into thermoplastic and thermosetting types. Thermoplastic phenolic resins can be reshaped upon heating, offering flexibility and ease of processing, making them suitable for applications requiring repeated molding. Conversely, thermosetting phenolic resins harden permanently under heat and pressure, providing high durability and excellent thermal resistance, which are essential for structural and insulation applications in buildings. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and longevity of construction materials.</p></p>
