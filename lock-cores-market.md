<p><strong>The Lock Cores Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncombinated Cores</li><li>Combinated Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lock cores refer to the central mechanisms in locking systems, and the market for these cores is divided into two main types: uncombinated and combinable cores. Uncombinated cores are single-key systems that offer straightforward security and can be easily replaced, providing quick access for users. In contrast, combinable cores allow for multiple key configurations, enabling customized access controls. This flexibility makes combinable cores ideal for larger facilities needing varied security levels, as they can be reconfigured without replacing the entire system.</p></p>
