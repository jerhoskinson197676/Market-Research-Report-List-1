<p><ul><li>Single Component Type</li><li>Thermosetting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid epoxy capillary underfill market comprises two main types: single-component and thermosetting types. Single-component underfills are ready-to-use formulations that do not require mixing, offering ease of application and stable performance during processing. Thermosetting types, on the other hand, undergo a curing process upon exposure to heat or UV light, providing enhanced mechanical properties and thermal stability. Both types are crucial in electronics packaging, ensuring reliable adhesion and protection of components against moisture and thermal cycling.</p></p>
