<p><p>The force gauge market comprises two primary types: digital force gauges and mechanical force gauges. Digital force gauges utilize electronic sensors to measure and display force values, offering high accuracy and ease of use, often with features like data logging and connectivity. In contrast, mechanical force gauges rely on springs and dials for measurement, providing a more traditional, analog approach. Both types serve various industries, including manufacturing and quality control, helping to assess material strength and ensure product integrity.</p></p>
