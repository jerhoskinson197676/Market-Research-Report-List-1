<p><strong>The Remote Sensing Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Remote Sensing</li><li>Passive Remote Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote sensing technology market is divided into two main types: active and passive remote sensing. Active remote sensing involves the emission of energy, such as radar or laser pulses, to measure reflected signals, allowing for detailed analysis of surface features regardless of sunlight conditions. Conversely, passive remote sensing relies on natural energy, mainly sunlight, to capture reflected radiation from objects on Earth, making it suitable for applications like vegetation and land cover monitoring. Both types play crucial roles in environmental assessment and resource management.</p></p>
