<p><p>The ECG and multi-parameter monitoring devices market includes various types of equipment used for cardiac assessment. Resting ECG devices record the heart's electrical activity at rest, providing vital baseline information. Stress ECG monitors assess heart response during physical activity, helping identify potential cardiac issues under exertion. Holter monitors offer continuous ECG monitoring over 24-48 hours, capturing transient arrhythmias and irregularities. Together, these devices enhance cardiac care, enabling timely diagnosis and management of heart conditions.</p></p>
