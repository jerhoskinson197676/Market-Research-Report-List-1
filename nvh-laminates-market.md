<p><p>The NVH (Noise, Vibration, and Harshness) laminates market is categorized into three main types: metal laminates, foam laminates, and film laminates. Metal laminates utilize layers of metal to effectively dampen vibrations and reduce noise, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Foam laminates are lightweight and absorb sound, offering versatility in various industries. Film laminates, often used for their thin profiles, provide effective sound insulation and vibration reduction while being easy to incorporate into existing designs.</p></p>
