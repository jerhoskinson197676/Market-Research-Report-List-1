<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive airbag textile market consists primarily of two types: flat airbag fabric and OPW (One-Piece Woven) airbag fabric. Flat airbag fabric is traditionally used for various airbag designs, offering durability and consistent performance under impact. In contrast, OPW airbag fabric is a newer innovation, seamlessly integrating the airbag’s structure into a single piece, enhancing strength and minimizing the risk of seams tearing during deployment. Both types are crucial for ensuring passenger safety in vehicles.</p></p>
