<p><p>Double Block and Bleed (DBB) Plug Valves are designed to provide a safe and effective means of isolating sections of pipeline systems. This design is particularly valuable in applications where safety and environmental protection are paramount, ensuring that potential leaks can be monitored and managed.</p><p>The market for Double Block and Bleed Plug Valves is seeing notable growth, driven by increasing demand in sectors such as oil and gas, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals.
