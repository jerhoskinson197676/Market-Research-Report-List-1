<p><p>The optical fiber distribution box market comprises several types of optical splitters, including 1:4, 1:8, 1:16, and 1:32 optical splitters. Each type indicates the number of output fibers relative to a single input fiber, with 1:4 supporting four outputs, 1:8 providing eight outputs, and so forth, up to 1:32 for thirty-two outputs. These splitters are crucial for distributing optical signals in telecommunications and data networks, facilitating efficient signal management and connectivity in various infrastructures.</p></p>
