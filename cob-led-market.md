<p><ul><li>Organic Light Emitting Diode</li><li>Inorganic Light Emitting Diode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COB LED market is primarily divided into two types: Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diodes (ILED). OLEDs utilize organic compounds to emit light, offering flexible designs, improved color quality, and energy efficiency, making them ideal for displays and lighting fixtures. In contrast, ILEDs use inorganic materials, known for their durability, brightness, and long lifespan. They are commonly employed in general lighting and backlighting applications due to their reliability and performance in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
