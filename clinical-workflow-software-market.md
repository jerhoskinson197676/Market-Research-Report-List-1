<p><p>Clinical workflow software is essential for streamlining healthcare processes, and it primarily comes in two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises solutions are installed locally on the healthcare organization's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Cloud-based software, conversely, operates on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and reduced upfront costs.
