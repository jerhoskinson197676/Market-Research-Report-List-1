<p><strong>The Water-Based Seed Colorants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dyes</li><li>Pigments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-based seed colorants market is primarily categorized into dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that penetrate the seed coat, providing vibrant hues and enhancing visibility for branding and identification. They often offer excellent colorfastness. Pigments, on the other hand, are insoluble and are used to create a more opaque coating on seeds. They are known for their durability and resistance to environmental factors. Both types play a crucial role in the agricultural sector, aiding in seed treatment and marketing efforts.</p></p>
