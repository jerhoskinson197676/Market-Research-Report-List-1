<p><p>Unleaded solder paste is a crucial material in the electronics manufacturing industry, primarily used for surface mount technology (SMT) applications. The demand for unleaded solder paste has been driven by the push for lead-free manufacturing processes due to regulations such as RoHS (Restriction of Hazardous Substances) and WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment), as well as increasing consumer awareness regarding the safety and environmental impacts of electronics.</p><p>The Unleaded Solder Paste Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.3% during the forecast period.
