<p><p>The Rotary Joint market is primarily divided into two types: Single Circuit Rotary Joints and Double Circuit Rotary Joints. Single Circuit Rotary Joints handle one fluid circuit, typically used for simpler applications involving gases or liquids. In contrast, Double Circuit Rotary Joints manage two separate fluid circuits simultaneously, making them ideal for more complex systems that require the conveyance of different fluids or media. Both types play a crucial role in industrial applications, enabling smooth rotational movement and efficient fluid transfer.</p></p>
