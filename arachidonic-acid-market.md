<p><p>Arachidonic acid (ARA) is a polyunsaturated omega-6 fatty acid that plays a critical role in cellular functions, including inflammation, immunity, and cell signaling. It is a key component of cell membranes and serves as a precursor to bioactive lipid mediators like prostaglandins and leukotrienes. ARA is naturally found in animal-based foods and is also synthesized in the human body. It is widely used in infant formula, dietary supplements, pharmaceuticals, and animal feed due to its health benefits, particularly in brain development and immune support.</p><p>The Arachidonic Acid Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.80% during the forecast period.
