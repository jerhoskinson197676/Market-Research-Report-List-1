<p><p>Standalone smart thermostats are devices that regulate home temperature independently, without needing integration with broader smart home systems. Wireless smart thermostats use Wi-Fi or other wireless protocols for connectivity, offering flexibility in installation and remote control via apps. Both types aim to enhance energy efficiency and user convenience, catering to different installation preferences and home setups.</p></p>
