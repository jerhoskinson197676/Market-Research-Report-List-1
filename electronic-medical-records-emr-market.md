<p><strong>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Client Server Setups</li><li>Cloud-based Setups</li><li>Hybrid Setups</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Medical Records (EMR) market consists of three primary setups: client-server, cloud-based, and hybrid. Client-server setups involve local servers hosting the EMR software, offering robust control over data but requiring significant IT resources. Cloud-based setups store EMRs on remote servers, providing flexibility, ease of access, and reduced maintenance burdens. Hybrid setups combine both models, allowing providers to have data on local servers while utilizing cloud resources for additional storage and accessibility, balancing control and scalability effectively.</p></p>
