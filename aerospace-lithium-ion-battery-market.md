<p><p>The aerospace lithium-ion battery market encompasses several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt (Li-NMC) offers high energy density and stability, making it popular for aircraft. Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP) is known for safety and long cycle life. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) provides high energy density but has limited thermal stability. Lithium Titanate Oxide (LTO) excels in fast charging and long life but has lower energy density. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) balances safety and performance, while Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminium Oxide (NCA) combines high energy density with thermal stability, ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
