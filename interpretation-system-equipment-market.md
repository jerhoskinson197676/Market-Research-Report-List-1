<p><p>The Interpretation System Equipment market is primarily divided into two types: wired and wireless systems. Wired systems utilize physical cables to connect interpreters and listeners, offering stable audio quality and low interference. However, they can limit mobility. In contrast, wireless systems use radio signals for communication, providing greater flexibility and freedom of movement. These systems often include portable receivers and transmitters, making them ideal for diverse environments such as conferences, courtrooms, and international events where mobility is crucial.</p></p>
