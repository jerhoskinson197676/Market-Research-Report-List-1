<p><p>The water chiller market comprises several types, each suited for specific cooling needs. Screw chillers utilize rotary screws to compress refrigerants, offering high efficiency for medium to large applications. Centrifugal chillers use centrifugal force for cooling, making them ideal for large-scale industrial uses due to their high cooling capacities. Reciprocating chillers operate using pistons for cooling, suitable for smaller applications. Other market types may include absorbed chillers and thermoelectric chillers, catering to niche or specialized cooling requirements.</p></p>
