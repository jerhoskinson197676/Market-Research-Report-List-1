<p><p>Thin film metrology systems are categorized based on the optical properties and thickness of the films they analyze. Opaque films, such as metals, absorb light and require techniques that measure their thickness and uniformity. Transparent films, including polymers and glass, allow light to pass through, necessitating different measurement approaches like reflectance or transmission. Thick films, typically over a few micrometers, require specialized tools for accurate profiling. Other films may include hybrid types or coatings with unique properties, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
