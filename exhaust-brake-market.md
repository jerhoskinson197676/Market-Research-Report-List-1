<p><p>The exhaust brake market can be categorized into two primary types: exhaust pipe exhaust brakes and engine exhaust brakes. Exhaust pipe exhaust brakes work by restricting the flow of exhaust gases through the exhaust system, creating back pressure that helps slow down the vehicle, primarily in heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, engine exhaust brakes utilize engine compression to generate braking force, effectively reducing the vehicle's speed while enhancing control and safety. Both types contribute to improved braking efficiency and reduced wear on traditional braking systems.</p></p>
