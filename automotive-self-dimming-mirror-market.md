<p><p>The automotive self-dimming mirror market is categorized into two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors, installed on vehicle exteriors, automatically adjust their tint to reduce glare from headlights of vehicles behind them, enhancing nighttime driving safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors, located inside the vehicle, serve a similar purpose, adjusting their brightness based on ambient light to minimize glare from rearview traffic, thereby improving driver comfort and visibility during various lighting conditions.</p></p>
