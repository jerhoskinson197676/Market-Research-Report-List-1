<p><p>The forklift seats market includes mechanical suspension seats and air suspension seats, designed to enhance operator comfort and safety. Mechanical suspension seats use springs or shock absorbers to reduce vibrations and impacts, offering durability and cost-effectiveness. Air suspension seats utilize air-filled cushions for superior vibration dampening and adjustable support, ideal for long shifts and uneven terrains. Both types cater to diverse operational needs, with mechanical seats being more affordable and air seats providing advanced comfort and ergonomic benefits.</p></p>
