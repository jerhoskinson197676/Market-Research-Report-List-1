<p><p>The PEX pipes market is segmented into three main types: PE-Xa, PE-Xb, and PE-Xc. PE-Xa, produced through the Engel method, offers superior flexibility and resistance to cracking. PE-Xb, manufactured by chemical cross-linking, is known for its excellent thermal stability and ease of installation. PE-Xc utilizes a silane method and provides good resistance to pressure and temperature fluctuations. Each type has distinct properties and applications, catering to various plumbing, heating, and industrial requirements while ensuring performance and durability.</p></p>
