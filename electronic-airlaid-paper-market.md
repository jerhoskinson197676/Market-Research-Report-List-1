<p><ul><li>Chemical Bonding</li><li>Thermal Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic airlaid paper is categorized mainly into two bonding types: chemical bonding and thermal bonding. Chemical bonding involves the use of adhesives or other chemicals to hold fibers together, providing enhanced strength and stability. In contrast, thermal bonding relies on heat to fuse the fibers without additional adhesives, resulting in a more environmentally friendly product. Each type serves different applications in industries such as hygiene, packaging, and filtration, offering varying properties such as softness, absorbency, and durability.</p></p>
