<p><p>An Automotive Charge Air Cooler (CAC) is a heat exchanger used in turbocharged and supercharged internal combustion engines to cool the compressed air before it enters the engine. By reducing the temperature of the intake air, the CAC increases air density, improving combustion efficiency, engine performance, and fuel economy while reducing emissions. It is a critical component in modern vehicles, especially in diesel and gasoline engines, to meet stringent emission regulations and enhance power output.</p><p>The Automotive Charge Air Cooler Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for fuel-efficient vehicles, stricter emission norms, and the rising adoption of turbocharged engines. <p><p>The Automotive Charge Air Cooler market includes two main types: Air-Cooled and Liquid-Cooled Charge Air Coolers. Liquid-Cooled Charge Air Coolers, on the other hand, utilize a liquid coolant to achieve the same cooling effect, offering better performance in compact spaces and high-temperature environments.
