<p><ul><li>Planar Monocrystalline Silicon Solar Cell</li><li>Grooved Buried Gate Electrode Monocrystalline Silicon Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The monocrystalline silicon solar cell market features two main types: planar monocrystalline silicon solar cells and grooved buried gate electrode monocrystalline silicon cells. Planar cells, the most common type, have a flat surface that enhances light absorption and efficiency. In contrast, grooved buried gate electrode cells incorporate a grooved design that improves electrical conductivity and reduces resistive losses. Both technologies aim to maximize energy conversion efficiency, catering to diverse applications in residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar power systems.</p></p>
