<p><strong>The AiP (Antenna in Package) Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LTCC</li><li>HDI</li><li>FOWLP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AiP (Antenna in Package) module market includes various packaging technologies such as LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic), HDI (High-Density Interconnect), and FOWLP (Fan-Out Wafer Level Packaging). LTCC offers excellent thermal management and miniaturization for high-frequency applications. HDI enhances interconnect density and reduces the size of the modules, catering to compact devices. FOWLP enables efficient use of space by incorporating antennas directly on the chip, making it ideal for advanced mobile and IoT applications, driving innovation in connectivity solutions.</p></p>
