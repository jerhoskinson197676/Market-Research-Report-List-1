<p><strong>The Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Transmission</li><li>Pulse Echo</li><li>Phased Array Pulse Echo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machines are categorized into three primary types: Through Transmission, Pulse Echo, and Phased Array Pulse Echo. Through Transmission involves sending ultrasonic waves through a test material, measuring their arrival time to assess thickness or detect flaws. Pulse Echo utilizes the reflection of ultrasonic waves from material boundaries to identify defects, providing depth information. Phased Array Pulse Echo systems enhance resolution and focus by controlling multiple ultrasonic elements, enabling detailed inspections of complex geometries and varying material conditions.</p></p>
