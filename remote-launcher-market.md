<p><p>The Remote Launcher market can be categorized into two main types: unidirectional and two-way. In contrast, a two-way market facilitates interactive communication, allowing users to send commands and receive feedback or data from the remote device, enhancing control and monitoring capabilities for complex applications.</p></p>
