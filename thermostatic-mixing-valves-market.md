<p><p>The thermostatic mixing valves market comprises two main types: Master Thermostatic Mixing Valves and Point of Use Thermostatic Mixing Valves. Master Thermostatic Mixing Valves regulate water temperature for entire systems or multiple outlets, ensuring consistent flow and safety, making them ideal for larger applications like commercial settings. In contrast, Point of Use Thermostatic Mixing Valves are installed close to a specific outlet, allowing precise temperature control for individual fixtures, enhancing user comfort in residential environments.</p></p>
