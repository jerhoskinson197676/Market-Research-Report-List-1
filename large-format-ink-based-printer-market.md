<p><strong>The Large Format Ink-based Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous ink</li><li>Solvent ink</li><li>UV-cured ink</li><li>Latex ink</li><li>Dye sublimation ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Large Format Ink-based Printer market comprises several ink types, each catering to specific applications. Aqueous ink is water-based, ideal for indoor prints with vibrant colors but limited outdoor durability. Solvent ink offers weather resistance, making it suitable for outdoor signage. UV-cured ink provides high durability and quick curing under UV light, while Latex ink combines eco-friendliness with flexibility, suitable for various materials. Dye sublimation ink is used for transferring designs onto substrates like fabric, ensuring vivid, high-quality prints.</p></p>
