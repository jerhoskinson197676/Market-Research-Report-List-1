<p><p>The hydrodesulfurization catalyst market is categorized into load type and non-load type. Load type catalysts are designed to adhere additional metal components, enhancing activity and selectivity in sulfur removal from hydrocarbons. They typically offer higher performance but may involve complex preparation. Non-load type catalysts, on the other hand, consist of a single active phase, simplifying production and application. Each type serves distinct needs in refining processes, balancing efficiency and operational considerations in the hydrodesulfurization of fuels.</p></p>
