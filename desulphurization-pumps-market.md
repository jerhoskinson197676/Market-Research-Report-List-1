<p><p>The desulphurization pumps market includes two primary types: vertical desulphurization pumps and horizontal desulphurization pumps. Vertical desulphurization pumps are designed for applications with limited floor space and are typically more efficient in handling corrosive fluids. They offer advantages in terms of suction lift and reduce the risk of cavitation. In contrast, horizontal desulphurization pumps provide easier maintenance and higher flow capacities, making them suitable for larger industrial applications. Both types are essential for efficient desulphurization processes across various industries.</p></p>
