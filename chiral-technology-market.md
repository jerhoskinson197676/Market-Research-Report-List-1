<p><ul><li>Traditional Separation Method</li><li>Asymmetric Preparation Method</li><li>Biological Separation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chiral technology market comprises three main types: Traditional Separation Methods, Asymmetric Preparation Methods, and Biological Separation Methods. Traditional separation methods involve physical techniques like crystallization and chromatography to isolate chiral compounds. Asymmetric preparation methods focus on synthesizing chiral molecules through specific chemical reactions that favor one enantiomer. Biological separation methods utilize biological systems, such as enzymes or microorganisms, to selectively produce or separate chiral compounds, leveraging their inherent specificity in catalysis and recognition, thus enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
