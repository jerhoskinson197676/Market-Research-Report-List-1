<p><p>The Omni Antenna market features various types, including monopole antennas, dipole antennas, and others. Monopole antennas consist of a single radiating element and are typically mounted on a ground plane, providing omnidirectional radiation. Dipole antennas, consisting of two elements, also offer omnidirectional patterns but are usually more efficient in certain applications. Other types can include diverse configurations such as circular, wideband, and specialized antennas designed for specific frequencies, enhancing connectivity in telecommunications, broadcasting, and wireless applications.</p></p>
