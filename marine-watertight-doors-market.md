<p><ul><li>Sliding Watertight Doors</li><li>Hinged Watertight Doors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine watertight doors market consists of two main types: sliding watertight doors and hinged watertight doors. Sliding watertight doors are designed to save space and provide easy access, often utilized in areas where movement is frequent. Hinged watertight doors feature traditional swing-opening mechanisms and are commonly found in bulkheads and compartments where maximum sealing is essential. Both types play a crucial role in preventing water ingress in marine environments, ensuring safety and structural integrity of vessels.</p></p>
