<p><ul><li>IR-based Motion-controlled Remotes</li><li>RF-based Motion-controlled Remotes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motion-controlled remote market is primarily divided into two categories: IR-based and RF-based remotes. IR-based motion-controlled remotes use infrared signals to detect user movements, requiring a direct line of sight to function effectively. In contrast, RF-based motion-controlled remotes utilize radio frequency signals, allowing for greater flexibility and range, as they do not require a direct line of sight. Both types enhance user interaction with devices, particularly in home entertainment settings, offering intuitive control through gestures.</p></p>
