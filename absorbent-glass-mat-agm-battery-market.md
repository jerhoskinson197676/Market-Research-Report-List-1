<p><p>Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) batteries are a type of lead-acid battery where the electrolyte is absorbed in a fiberglass mat, allowing for improved performance and maintenance-free operation. AGM batteries are known for their high energy density, lower self-discharge rates, and superior vibration resistance, making them ideal for various applications, including automotive, renewable energy systems, and uninterruptible power supplies (UPS).</p><p>The market for AGM batteries is anticipated to grow significantly, driven by increasing demand for efficient energy storage solutions and the rising adoption of electric vehicles. The trend towards renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power, is also enhancing the demand for AGM batteries, as they are favored for energy storage due to their long lifecycle and reliability.</p><p>Additional factors contributing to market growth include advancements in technology, increasing investments in research and development, and growing awareness of environmental sustainability.
