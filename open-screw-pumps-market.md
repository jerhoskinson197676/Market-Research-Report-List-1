<p><ul><li>Single Screw</li><li>Multi-screw</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The open screw pumps market is categorized into single screw and multi-screw types. Single screw pumps utilize a single helical screw to transport fluids, offering high efficiency and low maintenance, making them ideal for viscous liquids. Multi-screw pumps, on the other hand, employ multiple intermeshing screws to handle larger flow rates and provide excellent pressure stability. Both types find applications in various industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and food processing, due to their reliability and versatility in fluid transfer.</p></p>
