<p><p>Commercial auto fleet insurance encompasses two main market types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law, requiring businesses to carry a minimum level of coverage to protect against financial liabilities in the event of accidents. Optional insurance, on the other hand, provides additional coverage choices, allowing companies to customize their policies based on specific risks and needs. This includes coverage for damages, theft, and liability beyond the minimum requirements, offering greater protection for business operations.</p></p>
