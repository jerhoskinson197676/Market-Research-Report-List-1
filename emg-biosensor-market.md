<p><strong>The EMG Biosensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Biosensors</li><li>Intramuscular Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMG biosensor market is categorized into surface and intramuscular biosensors. Surface biosensors are non-invasive, placed on the skin to detect electrical signals from muscle activity, making them suitable for rehabilitation and movement analysis. In contrast, intramuscular biosensors are invasive and implanted within muscle tissue, offering higher fidelity in capturing electrical signals for applications like prosthetic control and neuromuscular research. Both types play crucial roles in medical diagnostics, rehabilitation, and the development of brain-computer interfaces.</p></p>
