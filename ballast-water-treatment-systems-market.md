<p><p>The ballast water treatment systems market is categorized into two primary methods: chemical and physical. Chemical methods involve the use of biocides or disinfectants to eliminate invasive organisms in ballast water, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. In contrast, physical methods utilize techniques such as filtration, ultraviolet light, or thermal processes to remove or inactivate pathogens without chemicals. Both methods aim to reduce the ecological impact of ballast water discharges, addressing the growing need for marine biodiversity protection and regulatory compliance.</p></p>
