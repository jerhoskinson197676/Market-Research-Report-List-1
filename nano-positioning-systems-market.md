<p><ul><li>Continuous Control</li><li>Point to Point Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nano-positioning systems are classified into two main market types: continuous control and point-to-point control. Continuous control systems provide smooth, ongoing adjustments to achieve precise positioning over a range of motion, suitable for applications requiring constant monitoring, such as microscopy and semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, point-to-point control systems focus on moving between defined positions with high accuracy, often used in scenarios like robotic assembly or optical alignment, where specific target locations are prioritized over fluid movement.</p></p>
