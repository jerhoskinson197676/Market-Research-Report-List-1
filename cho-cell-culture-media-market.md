<p><p>The CHO cell culture media market comprises two primary types: basal cell culture media and feed cell culture media. Basal cell culture media provide essential nutrients, vitamins, and salts necessary for cell survival and growth, serving as the foundation for cell culture. In contrast, feed cell culture media supplement the basal media by adding additional nutrients, growth factors, and precursors to enhance cell productivity and viability during the cultivation process. Both types are vital for optimizing CHO cell growth in biotechnological applications.</p></p>
