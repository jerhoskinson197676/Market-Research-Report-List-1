Semi-parametric equalizers use a mix of fixed and adjustable parameters to optimize signal quality, making them versatile for various applications. In contrast, fully-parametric equalizers allow for complete customization of the signal processing parameters, providing enhanced control over signal adjustments. Each type serves distinct needs in improving video signal fidelity, particularly in high-speed transmission environments, ensuring minimal distortion and improved performance.</p></p>
