<p><p>The online diagram editor market can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based editors are hosted on remote servers, allowing access from any device with internet connectivity, promoting collaboration and real-time editing. On-premises editors, on the other hand, are installed locally on a user's hardware, providing more control over data security and customization but often requiring maintenance and updates. Both options cater to varied user needs, from individual professionals to large organizations.</p></p>
