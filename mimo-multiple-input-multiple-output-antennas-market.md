<p><p>The MIMO (Multiple Input, Multiple Output) antennas market is primarily divided into two segments: Sub-6 GHz antennas and mmWave antennas. Sub-6 GHz antennas are designed for lower frequency bands, offering broader coverage and improved penetration, making them suitable for various applications, including 4G and early 5G networks. In contrast, mmWave antennas operate at higher frequencies, enabling faster data rates and increased capacity but with limited range and reduced penetration. Each segment serves distinct use cases in modern wireless communication systems.</p></p>
