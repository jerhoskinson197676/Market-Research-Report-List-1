<p><p>Once-through boilers (OTB) are efficient steam generators that circulate water only once through the system, avoiding the need for a drum. In the market, OTBs are categorized into horizontal and vertical types. Horizontal OTBs have a wider footprint, making them suitable for larger installations, while vertical OTBs save space and are ideal for smaller settings. Both types are utilized across various industries, including power generation and process heating, for their efficiency and reduced maintenance requirements.</p></p>
