<p><p>The child safety helmet market includes three main types: full face helmets, open face helmets, and half helmets. Full face helmets provide maximum protection, covering the entire head and face, ideal for high-impact activities. Open face helmets offer a balance between protection and visibility, leaving the face exposed while still safeguarding the head. Half helmets are lightweight and provide minimal coverage, suitable for lower-risk environments. Each type caters to different safety needs and preferences, ensuring children are adequately protected during various activities.</p></p>
