<p><p>Fluorapatite is a naturally occurring mineral form of calcium phosphate with the chemical formula Ca₅(PO₄)₃F. It is a key component of tooth enamel and bones, making it significant in dental and medical applications. Fluorapatite is also used in the production of fertilizers, ceramics, and as a raw material in the manufacturing of phosphoric acid. Its unique properties, such as high thermal stability and resistance to acid, make it valuable in various industrial applications.</p><p>The Fluorapatite Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period.
