<p><p>The commercial vehicle mirror market is divided into two primary types: internal mirrors and external mirrors. Internal mirrors, typically installed inside the vehicle cabin, provide drivers with visibility of the rear space, enhancing safety and awareness. External mirrors, mounted on the vehicle's exterior, include side-view and rear-view mirrors that offer critical visibility of the surroundings, aiding in lane changes and parking. Both types are essential for improving operational safety and efficiency in commercial transportation.</p></p>
