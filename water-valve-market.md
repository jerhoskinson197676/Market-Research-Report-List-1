<p><ul><li>Quarter-Turn Valves</li><li>Multi-Turn Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water valve market encompasses several key types: quarter-turn valves, multi-turn valves, and control valves. Quarter-turn valves operate with a simple 90-degree rotation, providing quick on/off flow control, ideal for applications like shut-off. Multi-turn valves require several rotations to fully open or close, allowing for fine flow regulation and are typically used in throttling applications. Control valves continuously regulate flow and pressure in systems, utilizing various mechanisms to manage process variables effectively, ensuring optimal performance in diverse environments.</p></p>
