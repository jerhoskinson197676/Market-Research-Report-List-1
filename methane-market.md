<p><p>The methane market includes fermentation and gasification as key production methods. Gasification converts carbon-rich materials like biomass or coal into syngas, which is further processed to methane. Both methods contribute to renewable energy, reducing reliance on fossil fuels. Fermentation is widely adopted for waste management, while gasification offers scalability for industrial applications. Both markets align with sustainability goals, promoting cleaner energy and waste utilization.</p></p>
