<p><strong>The Textile Surfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic</li><li>Cationic</li><li>Non-ionic</li><li>Amphoteric/Zwitterionic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile surfactants market is categorized into four main types: anionic, cationic, non-ionic, and amphoteric/zwitterionic. Anionic surfactants are negatively charged, providing excellent cleaning properties and foaming capabilities, commonly used in detergents. Cationic surfactants carry a positive charge, offering antimicrobial properties and enhancing fabric softness. Non-ionic surfactants, with no charge, are versatile and gentle, suitable for a range of applications. Amphoteric or zwitterionic surfactants have both positive and negative charges, making them effective in varied pH conditions and enhancing stability.</p></p>
