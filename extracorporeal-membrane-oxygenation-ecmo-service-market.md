<p><ul><li>VA (Veno-Arterial) ECMO Services</li><li>VV (Veno-Venous) ECMO Services</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) services are categorized into two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO. VA ECMO is utilized for patients with both cardiac and respiratory failure, providing support by returning oxygenated blood to the body while simultaneously aiding the heart. Conversely, VV ECMO focuses solely on respiratory support for patients with severe lung issues, allowing for gas exchange without assisting the heart. Both services are crucial in critical care settings for life-saving interventions.</p></p>
