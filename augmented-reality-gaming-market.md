<p><ul><li>Head Mounted Display</li><li>Handheld Display</li><li>Spatial Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The augmented reality gaming market is categorized into three main types: Head Mounted Displays (HMDs), Handheld Displays, and Spatial Displays. HMDs, like VR headsets, provide immersive experiences by overlaying digital content in the user's field of vision. Handheld Displays, often smartphones or tablets, utilize cameras and screens to blend virtual elements with the real world. Spatial Displays create interactive environments, projecting virtual content into physical spaces, enhancing social interactions and gaming experiences. Each type caters to different user preferences and technological capabilities.</p></p>
