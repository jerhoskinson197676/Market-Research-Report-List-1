<p><p>The alkali-free glass substrate market comprises two primary types: alkali-free aluminosilicate glass and alkali-free borosilicate glass. Alkali-free aluminosilicate glass offers high thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications in electronics and high-performance devices. On the other hand, alkali-free borosilicate glass features excellent thermal shock resistance and is commonly used in laboratory and industrial glassware. Both types cater to sectors requiring superior durability and reliability, driving their demand in various high-tech applications.</p></p>
