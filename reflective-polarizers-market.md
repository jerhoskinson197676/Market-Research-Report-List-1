<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective polarizers are categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer polarizers consist of a single film that efficiently transmits and reflects polarized light. They are simpler and cost-effective for applications requiring basic polarization. Multi-layer polarizers, on the other hand, use multiple layers of materials to achieve enhanced performance, offering greater efficiency, transmission rates, and optical properties. This type is ideal for advanced applications in displays, optics, and imaging systems where superior polarization and light management are essential.</p></p>
