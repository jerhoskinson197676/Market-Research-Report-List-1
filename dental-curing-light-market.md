<p><p>The dental curing light market is primarily categorized into two types: LED lamps and halogen lamps. LED lamps are known for their efficiency, rapid curing capabilities, and longer lifespan. They emit specific wavelengths suitable for various dental materials, ensuring optimal polymerization. Halogen lamps, on the other hand, use a traditional bulb that produces a broad spectrum of light but can generate more heat, requiring more time for effective curing. Both types serve essential roles in dental procedures, impacting treatment outcomes and patient care.</p></p>
