<p><p>The quarter-turn actuator market is categorized into three main types: electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic actuators. Electric quarter-turn actuators utilize electric motors for precision control and are ideal for applications requiring fast, reliable operation. Pneumatic quarter-turn actuators rely on compressed air to provide quick action and are commonly used in environments where explosive conditions exist. Hydraulic quarter-turn actuators use pressurized fluid for greater force and are suitable for heavy-duty applications requiring high torque. Each type serves specific operational needs in various industries.</p></p>
