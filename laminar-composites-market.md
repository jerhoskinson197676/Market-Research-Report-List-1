<p><strong>The Laminar Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brazing</li><li>Coextrusion</li><li>Explosive Bonding</li><li>Roll Bonding</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminar composites are materials made by bonding multiple layers of different materials to achieve enhanced properties. The market types include:</p><p>1. **Brazing**: A process where layers are joined using a filler metal with a lower melting point, creating strong, heat-resistant bonds.  </p><p>2. **Coextrusion**: Layers are fused together by forcing materials through a die, ideal for creating uniform, multi-layered structures.  </p><p>3. **Explosive Bonding**: High-energy explosions bond layers, suitable for combining dissimilar metals with high strength.  </p><p>4. **Roll Bonding**: Layers are pressed and rolled together under high pressure, forming durable, lightweight composites.  </p><p>5. **Others**: Includes techniques like adhesive bonding or diffusion bonding, offering versatility for specific applications.</p></p>
