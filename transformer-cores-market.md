<p><ul><li>Steel Laminated Cores</li><li>Solid Cores</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transformer Cores market is primarily segmented into Steel Laminated Cores and Solid Cores. Steel Laminated Cores are composed of thin sheets of silicon steel, reducing eddy current losses and improving efficiency, making them popular in power transformers. Solid Cores, on the other hand, are made from a single piece of metal, typically used in smaller transformers due to their robustness. Each type serves different applications, influencing efficiency, weight, and production costs in the electrical industry.</p></p>
