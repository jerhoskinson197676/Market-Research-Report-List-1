<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical silicone adhesive market is divided into two main types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component adhesives cure upon exposure to moisture in the air, making them user-friendly and convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component adhesives require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, offering enhanced strength and durability. Each type caters to specific medical device requirements, such as skin compatibility and bonding efficiency, ensuring optimal performance in healthcare applications.</p></p>
