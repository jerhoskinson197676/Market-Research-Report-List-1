Ion-exchange Resin Filters rely on resin beads to exchange ions, effectively removing dissolved impurities like heavy metals or salts, commonly used in water treatment. Both filters are integral in environmental and industrial processes, offering efficient, targeted solutions for contamination control and purification needs.</p></p>
