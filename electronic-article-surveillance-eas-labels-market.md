<p><strong>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acousto-Magnetic (AM) EAS</li><li>Microwave EAS</li><li>Electro-Magnetic EAS</li><li>UHF, Gen 2 RFID EAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electronic Article Surveillance (EAS) labels market comprises several types. Acousto-Magnetic (AM) EAS utilizes magnetic signals to detect stolen items, while Microwave EAS employs microwave technology for similar purposes. Electro-Magnetic EAS operates using electromagnetic fields to trigger alarms. UHF EAS offers improved detection range and inventory management through ultra-high frequency signals. Gen 2 RFID EAS combines radio frequency identification with security features, enabling real-time tracking and improved anti-theft measures. Each type addresses specific retail security needs.</p></p>
